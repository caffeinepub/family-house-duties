# Color Scheme Suggestions for Family Task Manager

## 1. **Ocean Breeze** (Selected Default)
A calming, modern palette inspired by coastal waters and sky. Features deep teal as primary with coral accents for warmth. Perfect for a family app that needs to feel both professional and approachable.
- Primary: Deep Teal (180° hue)
- Accent: Coral/Salmon (15° hue)
- Success: Seafoam Green (160° hue)
- Destructive: Warm Red (25° hue)

## 2. Forest Harmony
An earthy, natural palette with forest green primary and golden amber accents. Evokes growth, stability, and warmth—ideal for family organization and nurturing household tasks.
- Primary: Forest Green (140° hue)
- Accent: Golden Amber (60° hue)
- Success: Lime Green (120° hue)
- Destructive: Brick Red (20° hue)

## 3. Lavender Dusk
A sophisticated, gentle palette with soft purple primary and peachy accents. Modern and calming, perfect for reducing stress around household management.
- Primary: Soft Purple (280° hue)
- Accent: Peach (40° hue)
- Success: Mint Green (150° hue)
- Destructive: Rose Red (355° hue)

---

**Selected for Implementation:** Ocean Breeze

This scheme provides excellent contrast, feels fresh and modern without being generic, and works beautifully in both light and dark modes. The teal primary avoids the overused blue while remaining professional, and the coral accent adds warmth and energy.
